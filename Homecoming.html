Homecoming 2015
Logo Design

Summary

These are two designs submitted to the 2015 Homecoming Logo Competition, one of which was selected for finals. The tagline was "Buzzin' Out of This World", a space-themed logo with Buzz (Georgia Tech's mascot) flying into the universe.

Client

Georgia Institute of Technology

Team

Amy Zhen

Inspiration

I mindmapped ideas of Buzz riding a rocket, becoming an astronaut, and flying around the universe. I expanded on the idea of Buzz could be sitting on the rocketship. The elements of Buzz, rocket, fire trail, and tagline all integrate seamlessly into the theme of buzzing out of this world.

Design

This final design brought life to Buzz as he is propelled into space by this powerful rocketship. The color of the ship is modest, to draw the eye towards the bright tagline in the rocket trail.
This design included Buzz flying around Earth, with the tagline following the same curve of his trail. I used futuristic font for the tagline and a still-capture of Buzz flying to resemble retro space exploration.
