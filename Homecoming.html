These are two designs submitted to the 2015 Homecoming Logo Competition, one of which was selected for finals. The tagline was "Buzzin' Out of This World", a space-themed logo with Buzz (Georgia Tech's mascot) flying into the universe.
Georgia Institute of Technology
Amy Zhen

Inspiration

I explored ideas about space using the mindmap below, drawing sketches of Buzz riding a rocket, becoming an astronaut, and incorporating him in the universe. I enjoyed the idea of Buzz riding lightspeed on a rocketship, and expanded this concept by developing more sketches.
I sketched out different ways Buzz could be sitting on the rocketship. The elements of Buzz, rocket, fire trail, and tagline all integrate seamlessly into the theme of buzzing out of this world.
I then explored the various ship designs and rocket trails. I turned to real spaceship designs for inspiration, pulling only the necessary components that make a spaceship one of a kind. I shifted and combined elements to product a rocket that was sleek by its pointed thrusters and detailed by its differently sized window apertures.

Design

This final design brought life to Buzz as he is propelled into space by this powerful rocketship. The color palette for the ship was modest in order to emphasize the bright tagline. I developed this design at an angle so that the eye would flow continuously from the tagline to Buzz enjoying his ride atop the rocket. This clean graphic can be distinguished from afar, compared to the other designs I had that might have distracted rather than complemented.
This design included Buzz flying around Earth, with the tagline following the same curve of his trail. It was a simpler design using only several colors, but the elements fit together seamlessly. I used futuristic font for the tagline and a “still” of flying Buzz to resemble retro space exploration. Overall, the design conveyed the message in a clear manner, allowing viewers to focus first on Buzz and then on space travel.
