Christmas Card Project
Illustration

Goal

I designed and crafted handmade Christmas cards for my friends and family. Several of them were lasercut from cardstock and personalized with a handwritten letter.

Inspiration

I decided to create my own cards that was unique in its design and production. Passionate in uniting design and technology, I decided to incorporate laser-cutting as the final touch.

I explored color and illustration, sketching details in type to create fun and quirky graphics to illustrate the joyfulness of the holidays.

I also practiced my typography and layout skills, turning common greetings like “Merry Christmas” into active illustrations within the shapes. Before laser-cutting, I made sure there were no extraneous elements that might create unintentional cuts. Designing for the laser cutter taught me invaluable manufacturing techniques and considerations.
