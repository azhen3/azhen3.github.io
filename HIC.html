Forge Report
2015 Health Innovation Challenge

Goal

Forge, a health tech NGO, released the Health Innovation Challenge Event Report for 2015. This report allowed investors and potential companies to understand Forge's vision and accomplishments.
View Report

Inspiration

I referred to Forge’s brand identity to develop the report. I wanted the document to reflect the innovative and unique aspects of Forge, a community of entrepreneurs, clinicians, investors, and engineers coming together to disrupt healthcare.

I was inspired by current infographic designs to understand how to visualize data effectively and applied these techniques to display the event’s demographic information. The final report I developed not only describes the milestones of the event, but also represents Forge’s vision as a health-driven community.
