Forge Salon
Flyer Graphic Design

Summary

I am the graphic designer for Forge, an Atlanta-based digital health organization focused on connecting physicians, entrepreneurs, and engineers to disrupt healthcare. I create the monthly event flyers for networking events and panel discussions.

Client

Forge

Inspiration

Forge is a community of individuals from many backgrounds- healthcare, business, engineering, just to name a few. We abstracted this concept of bridging relationships to create a visual graphic, one composed of geometrical pieces consisting of various hues, which transformed into Forge’s latest brand identity. I wanted to portray this message through the event flyers by using the same shapes but changing the color palette for each event.
