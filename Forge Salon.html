Forge Salon
Flyer Graphic Design

Goal

I was the graphic designer for Forge, an Atlanta-based digital health organization focused on connecting physicians, entrepreneurs, and engineers to disrupt healthcare. I create the monthly event flyers for networking events and panel discussions.

Client

Forge

Inspiration

I abstracted Forge's concept of bridging relationships to create a visual graphic, one composed of geometrical pieces consisting of various hues, which transformed into its official brand identity. I portrayed this message through the event flyers by using the same shapes but explored different the color schemes for each event.
