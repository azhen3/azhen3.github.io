AMY ZHEN

Visual & User Interaction Designer

I am a Biomedical Engineer senior minoring in Industrial Design, who is passionate in improving human life through the integration of advanced technology, engineering, and human-centered design. I have over three years of experience in health-focused engineering, machining/ prototyping, product design, and marketing, all of which I believe are necessary to build better technologies to prolong human life.

In my free time I love making gifts for my friends, whether it be hand-drawn birthday cards or 3D printed desk toys. I love to see how my passion for design, art, music, dance, tech, and engineering can interact together and inspire others.
